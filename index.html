How It Works

  1. Understand Your Environment — We start by mapping your domains, cloud accounts, applications, and data flows — giving you a clear picture of what needs protection.
  2. Assess Your Security Posture — We review access controls, backups, cloud settings, external exposure, logging, and incident readiness through lightweight, non-intrusive checks.
  3. Identify & Prioritize Risks — Findings are translated into business-level risks, sorted by impact and effort, so you know exactly what to fix first.
  4. Deliver a Clear Action Plan — You receive a practical 30–60 day roadmap, PDPL/GDPR-aligned security insights (non-legal), and a clean report ready for management, partners, or regulators.
